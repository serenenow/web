FAQ

SereneNow is an all-in-one platform designed specifically for mental health professionals in India to manage their practice efficiently. It combines client management, appointment scheduling, payment processing, and secure data storage in one seamless application.

SereneNow is designed for mental health professionals in India including psychologists, psychiatrists, counselors, and therapists who want to streamline their practice management and provide better service to their clients.

SereneNow offers two apps: the Expert app for mental health professionals and the Client app for their patients. The platform allows seamless communication, appointment scheduling, payment processing, and secure data management between both parties.

You only need a smartphone or computer with internet access. Our platform works on both iOS and Android devices, as well as through any modern web browser.

SereneNow simplifies practice management by bringing all essential tools into one platform, saving you time and reducing administrative burden. It's designed specifically for the Indian mental health context, with features that address local needs and challenges.
